# Design Partner Pilot Runbook

## Pre-Pilot Check

Before inviting design partners, confirm:

- organizer intake does not advance after only idea plus city
- host, creative, venue, and fan paths all render real Sagasan replies
- brief review shows partial vs ready states clearly
- talent search stays gated until the brief is ready
- no outreach, terms, or booking actions can happen automatically
- no ticketing contradiction is visible

## Transcript Review Focus

Review internal dogfood and pilot transcripts for:

- correct persona routing
- high-signal follow-up quality
- producer voice
- readiness gating quality
- clean brief extraction
- safe boundaries

## Go / No-Go

Do not move into design partner testing if:

- organizers can reach planning or staffing with low-signal context
- the brief is marked complete too early
- downstream pages show duplicated transcript text
- external action boundaries fail

## External Action Reminder

Even in pilot:

- no candidate outreach without human review
- no booking guarantees
- no group-chat creation
- no ticketing or payment handling by Sagasan
