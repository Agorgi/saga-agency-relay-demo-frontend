# Sagasan Dogfood Scoring Rubric

## Green

- 90% or more of flows render real Sagasan replies
- 90% or more persona classification accuracy
- 90% or more CTA and handoff success
- no overpromise failures
- no stale CTA P1 issues
- average clarity score is at least 4 out of 5
- average usefulness score is at least 4 out of 5

## Yellow

- minor copy issues
- occasional vague next question
- one or two handoff rough edges
- no safety issues

## Red

- wrong persona in common flows
- CTA loses context
- paid-work, booking, or ticketing overpromise
- stale CTA causes the wrong route
- generic fallback appears in a normal success path
- internal system details are shown to users

## Design partner readiness

Sagasan is ready for design partner testing when:

- at least 20 internal conversations have been reviewed
- no red issues are open
- most yellow issues are triaged
- the top 5 copy improvements are identified
- the top 5 UX improvements are identified
