# Design Partner Feedback Questions

Use these questions after a private design-partner session. Capture answers in
`/admin/pilot-feedback` or the project detail feedback panel. Do not include raw
phone numbers, secrets, payment details, ticketing details, production Saga app
data, or sensitive legal/medical information in feedback notes.

## Core Questions

1. Was Saga easy to understand?
2. Did Saga ask the right next question?
3. Did the tone feel friendly and producer-like?
4. Did you trust the next step?
5. Was anything confusing?
6. Did Saga overpromise anything?
7. What did you expect it to do that it did not?
8. Would you use this again?
9. Would you recommend it to another creator or organizer?
10. What would make it feel more useful?

## Optional Follow-Ups

- Which first message did you send?
- Did you know whether you were in project, creator/gig, or interest-check
  mode?
- Did the reply feel too vague, too long, or too confident?
- Did you understand that Saga was not promising bookings, paid opportunities,
  confirmed teams, venue access, ticket outcomes, or event execution?
- What would you change before inviting the next tester?

## Suggested Admin Scoring

Score each from 1 to 5:

- Clarity.
- Tone.
- Trust.
- Usefulness.
- Next-step confidence.
- Safety/boundary clarity.

Use categories such as `intake_quality`, `tone`, `trust`, `confusion`,
`usefulness`, `matching`, `safety`, `bug`, or `other`.
